We don’t hide, we stand tall in front of challenge

Invariant Telecom is a major concern of the Microtrade Group who began its humble journey in 2014. We are a software development company based in Dhaka, Bangladesh who specializes in both web and mobile applications. Our products are aimed at a variety of industries including banking, telecommunications and digital marketing.
